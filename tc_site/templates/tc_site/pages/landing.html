What are Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy statements?

Terms & Conditions and privacy policy statements are important agreements a website should have. They set out what is expected from both you and your users. Privacy policy explains to users how their personal data will be used, kept safe, and how they can excercise their rights over their personal data.
